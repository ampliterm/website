## Overview

The Intel Feed aggregates discrete market signals and surfaces them in chronological order, filtered to instruments on your watchlist. Signal types include earnings beats/misses, analyst rating changes, unusual options flow, and macro data releases.

## Signal types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `EARNINGS` | Beat or miss vs. consensus estimate |
| `ANALYST` | Rating change or price target update |
| `FLOW` | Unusual options activity |
| `MACRO` | Fed decisions, CPI, NFP, and similar data releases |
| `NEWS` | Material company news filtered for relevance |

## Attaching signals to entries

Click the pin icon on any signal to attach it to an open journal entry. Attached signals appear in the entry's timeline and are included in simulation context when you run a backtest against that entry.
