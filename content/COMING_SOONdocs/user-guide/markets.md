## Watchlists

Create and manage watchlists from the Markets sidebar. Watchlists drive the Intel Feed filter — only instruments on your active watchlist surface signals. You can have multiple watchlists and toggle between them.

## Symbol search

Press `⌘K` from anywhere in the terminal to open the command palette. Type a ticker symbol to jump to its detail view, or type a company name to search.

## Market overview

The Markets overview page displays a sector heatmap, major index performance, and your watchlist summary. Use `G M` to navigate here from anywhere in the terminal.
